Kendall Jenner's Street Style

Kendall Jenner has a stylish off-duty style that is inspirational to fashionistas all over the world. This site serves as an archive of her best looks so that you can bring some of Kendall's style into your own! Enjoy.

Kendall's Looks

Casual Chic

Look casual but sleek in a knit crop top, high-waisted trousers and some squeaky-clean white sneakers. Shop the look.

The top knot reigns

Get that DGAF look with an oversized jean jacket over a satin-y shift dress or t-shirt dress. Top it off with some shades and a top knot. Half-up, half-down for the ultra trendy! ;)

Edgy Look

Ughh! We love how Kendall rocks the grunge rocker chic vibe. It's all about layers and leather jackets and black. Yes please!

Classic Look

Dayumm Kendall! Back at it again with the crop tops! Pair a plain crop top with some mom jeans, girlfriend jeans, or straight leg ankle jeans and some classic loafers or slides and look utterly timeless.
